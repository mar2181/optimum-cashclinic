Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Our Commitment

Optimum Health & Wellness Clinic is committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your personal and health information. We adhere to the highest standards of data protection and clinical ethics.

2. Information Collection

We collect information necessary to provide clinical care, including medical history, contact details, and payment information. This data is collected through secure forms and clinical consultations.

3. Data Security

Your data is stored in encrypted systems that comply with industry standards. Access is restricted to authorized medical personnel involved in your care.

4. Patient Rights

You have the right to request access to your medical records, amend inaccuracies, and receive an accounting of disclosures. To exercise these rights, please contact our privacy officer at info@optimumclinicra.com.
