Walk In, Walk Out, Feel Better

Optimum Clinic operates on a simple premise: urgent care should be available when you actually need it, not just during banker's hours. Extended evening and weekend hours mean you can get seen for everything from flu symptoms and sprains to infections and minor injuries without rearranging your entire life to do it.

No appointments necessary. Walk in, check in at the front desk, and get seen by a provider. The clinic is staffed with experienced medical professionals who handle the full spectrum of non-emergency conditions with the kind of efficiency that makes you wonder why you ever waited in an ER for something that did not require one.

What We Treat

The scope of services covers what most people actually need from healthcare on a given day: fevers, colds, flu, ear infections, strep throat, UTIs, minor cuts and lacerations, sprains, allergic reactions, rashes, eye infections, and work or school physicals. Lab work and rapid testing are available on-site, which means results come fast and treatment starts immediately.

For conditions that require a higher level of care, the team provides seamless referrals and can coordinate with your primary care physician to make sure nothing falls through the cracks. You get treated and you get connected, not just handed a bill and sent on your way.
