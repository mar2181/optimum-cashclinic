Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our premier after-hours medical services. We believe in informed care and complete transparency.

Do you accept insurance?

We are a direct-pay cash clinic. While we do not bill insurance directly, this allows us to offer transparent, affordable pricing and longer consultation times without the interference of insurance networks.

What are your operating hours?

We specialize in after-hours care, operating from 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Friday, and select weekend hours. Perfect for busy professionals and families.

Do I need an appointment?

While we do welcome walk-ins, we recommend scheduling an appointment online or via phone to minimize your wait time and ensure the best experience.

What if I need laboratory tests?

We offer many rapid tests on-site. For more complex blood work, we coordinate with local high-end laboratories at pre-negotiated cash rates for our patients.
